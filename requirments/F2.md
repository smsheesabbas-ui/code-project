# Feature 2: Business Entity Structuring Layer

## Overview

The Business Entity Structuring Layer replaces traditional accounting chart-of-accounts with intuitive, business-friendly entities.

Instead of:

* Ledger codes
* Account trees
* Double-entry complexity

We use:

* Customers
* Suppliers
* Revenue Streams
* Expense Categories
* Tags

The goal is clarity over compliance.

A business owner should understand where money comes from and where it goes without learning accounting.

---

# EPIC 1: Intelligent Entity Recognition

## Objective

Automatically identify and assign transactions to business entities such as customers and suppliers using AI-driven classification.

## Business Value

Reduces manual categorization effort and removes accounting intimidation.

## Scope

* AI-based entity extraction from transaction descriptions
* Customer and supplier creation
* User correction and feedback loop
* Learning from corrections

## Functional Requirements

* Detect potential customer/supplier from transaction text
* Suggest entity assignment with confidence score
* Allow manual correction
* Auto-create new entities when necessary
* Store entity history per user

## Non-Functional Requirements

* Classification accuracy â‰¥ 85% (initial)
* Learning improves over time
* No accounting terminology in UI
* Response time < 2 seconds
