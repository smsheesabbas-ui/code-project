# Feature 3: AI Financial Intelligence Engine

## Overview

The AI Financial Intelligence Engine processes structured transactions and provides actionable, human-friendly insights to micro-business owners.

Instead of raw numbers, users receive:

* Smart classifications
* Spending patterns
* Risk alerts
* Forecasts
* Personalized recommendations

The goal is to turn financial data into intelligence that a non-accountant can act upon immediately.
